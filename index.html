Greek Salad

Ingredients

Dressing

  • ¼ cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • ¼ teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • ¼ teaspoon sea salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper

For the salad

  • 1 cucumber, cut lengthwise
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 cups halved cherry tomatoes
  • 5 ounces feta cheese, cut into cubes
  • ⅓ cup thinly sliced red onion
  • ⅓ cup pitted Kalamata olives
  • ⅓ cup fresh mint leaves

Instructions

  1. Make the dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, vinegar, garlic, oregano, mustard, salt, and several grinds of pepper.
  2. On a large platter, arrange the cucumber, green pepper, cherry tomatoes, feta cheese, red onions, and olives.
  3. Drizzle with the dressing and very gently toss.
  4. Sprinkle with a few generous pinches of oregano and top with the mint leaves.
  5. Season to taste and serve.
